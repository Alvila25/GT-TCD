Our Story

Greenhouse Telehealth TCD was founded to bridge healthcare gaps in Chad. We deliver quality medical care to underserved communities through innovative telehealth solutions. Our dedicated team of medical and tech professionals drives a movement to make healthcare accessible for all.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

Bridge Chad's healthcare gap with telemedicine, enhancing access in remote areas, improving maternal and child health, and empowering communities through education for sustainable healthcare.

Our Vision

Envision a Chad where everyone has access to quality healthcare, reducing disparities, lowering maternal and child mortality, and minimizing preventable diseases.

Greenhouse Telehealth TCD Brief

Background

Greenhouse Telehealth TCD delivers essential healthcare to Chad's underserved communities. With high maternal (856/100,000) and child (119/1,000) mortality rates (UNICEF 2023), our telemedicine connects patients with professionals, improving outcomes.

Problem Statement

Chad faces healthcare challenges: poor infrastructure, 0.4 physicians per 10,000 people (WHO 2023), and 40% poverty (World Bank 2023). Only 30% live near health facilities, and 40% of children face malnutrition (UNICEF 2023). Our telehealth solution addresses these gaps.

Proposed Solution

Our digital health platform uses telemedicine, mobile tools, and education to connect patients with professionals, delivering affordable, culturally sensitive care to Chad's remote regions.

Objectives

- Expand access with telehealth hubs
- Improve maternal and child health
- Train local health workers
- Use data for real-time health insights
- Promote inclusive, equitable care

Scope

Offers telemedicine, health education, mobile solutions, training, and community support for Chad's underserved regions.

Target Audience

Serves patients, healthcare providers, and public health organizations in Chad's remote and underserved areas.

Partnerships

Collaborates with Chad's Ministry of Health, WHO, NGOs, and Tech for Good Foundation.

Key Attributes

Focuses on convenience, accessibility, affordability, and security to empower users with trusted healthcare.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • What is Telehealth?

      Telehealth uses digital tools like video, chat, or messaging to deliver remote clinical and non-clinical healthcare services, connecting patients with healthcare professionals efficiently.

    • How do I book a consultation?

      Log in to your account, browse available doctors, select a convenient time slot, and confirm your virtual appointment through our secure platform.

    • Is my data secure?

      Yes, we use end-to-end encryption, secure storage, and regular audits to ensure your personal and health data remains confidential and protected.

    • What services are available?

      We offer virtual care, mental health support, maternal and child health services, chronic disease management, telepharmacy, medical education, and emergency triage.
